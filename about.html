About The Crazy Florist

The Crazy Florist is a mobile flower shop established in 2025. The shop operates from a vehicle and travels to towns and highway stops to sell fresh, affordable flowers. The business was started out of a love for flowers and a mission to make beautiful blooms accessible to everyone. The Crazy Florist continues to spread joy by bringing its floral creations directly to the community through its mobile setup.

Goals

To reach more customers, share travel destinations, and showcase floral products online.

To inspire people to appreciate the simple joy and peace that flowers bring, and to make beautiful blooms available to anyone, anywhere.

Destinations

CrazyFlorist regularly visits Marilaque Tanay, and occasionally the Morong Highway. Follow our social pages to see our next stop.
